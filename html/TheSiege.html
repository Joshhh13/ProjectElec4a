The Siege

“The Siege” by Helen Dunmore is a powerful historical novel set during the brutal winter of the 1941–1942
Siege of Leningrad. The story follows Anna, a young woman forced to protect her family as food
disappears and the city freezes under relentless blockade. Dunmore shows how ordinary people fight to
survive, not only against hunger and cold but also against fear and hopelessness. Through Anna’s quiet
strength and her growing bond with Andrei, the novel reveals how love can become a form of resistance
in the darkest times. The book captures the claustrophobic atmosphere of a city cut off from the world,
