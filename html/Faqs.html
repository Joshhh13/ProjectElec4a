FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is a Library Management System?▶
A Library Management System is software designed to manage books, users, and borrowing efficiently.
Who can use this system?▶
Students, teachers, and librarians can use this system.
How can I search for a book?▶
You can search using the search bar by entering the book title, author, or keywords.
Can I reserve a book online?▶
Yes, you can reserve books through your user account online.
How does the system help librarians?▶
It automates book tracking, notifications, and inventory management for librarians.
Is it possible to track overdue books?▶
Yes. The system automatically notifies users about overdue books and helps librarians keep track of penalties or fines.
Can this system be customized for different schools or libraries?▶
Yes, it can be tailored to fit the specific needs of different libraries.
Does the platform support digital resources?▶
Yes, digital books, PDFs, and other resources can be managed.
Is the system user-friendly for first-time users?▶
Yes, the system is designed with a simple and intuitive interface for first-time users.
