Remain

“Remain” tells the story of Tate Donovan, a New York architect who visits Cape Cod to escape the grief of
losing his wife. While staying at a quiet coastal bed-and-breakfast, he begins experiencing strange and
supernatural events that force him to question what is real. As Tate uncovers secrets tied to the house and
its past, he realizes that the haunting may be connected to his own unresolved pain. The novel blends
romance and mystery, showing how love can remain present even after loss. In the end, Tate must
confront the truth if he hopes to heal and move forward.
